Who We Are

A decade of milsim, built on community

COALITION was founded in 2016 as an 18+ gaming community centred around ArmA. What started as a small group of friends grew into thousands of international players united by a passion for military simulation done right.

We run multiple weekly events across Arma Reforger, DayZ, War Thunder, DCS, and Squad — from tightly structured TVT missions to open community operations. COALITION is also a hub for mod creators; our frameworks and mission tools are used by communities well beyond our own.

Whether you're an experienced milsim player or someone curious about the genre for the first time, there's a place for you here.

Our Philosophy

Realism and fun go hand-in-hand

Whether or not you realise it, structure and having a good time are not opposites in a controlled milsim environment. COALITION was built on the belief that a well-run game makes for a better game — and that staff should always serve the players, not the other way around.

Fun is the priority

COALITION staff will never place milsim rules as a priority over having fun. Everyone is here to play military simulations — and to enjoy themselves doing it. No exceptions.

Structured, not strict

There are no real-world ranks, no drill and ceremony, no rules that bleed outside the game. In-game command structure exists because a military simulation needs it — not because we want a hierarchy.

International and open

Players from across the globe fly missions together every week. COALITION has always been 18+ and multi-national — the only requirement is that you respect the people around you.
