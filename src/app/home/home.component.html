ABOUT US


COALITION is an adult gaming community and milsim event host, which was founded in 2016, for ArmA and other milsim games. Formed by veterans, for veterans, the community has since grown to house thousands of international players across DayZ, War Thunder, Squad, Reforger, DCS, and any other multiplayer game which is empowered by community.

WHAT WE DO

ArmA Reforger


Moving on from ArmA 3, Reforger is the current stepping stone in COALITIONs central ArmA milsim gameplay experience. COALITION ArmA gameplay includes:
  • Organized and Structured Gameplay
  • Private campaigns
  • 128 player events
  • COALITION Fight Club
  • Public modded conflict servers
From making mods for the Reforger community to hosting events for both communities and individuals to enjoy, COALITION is the place for Reforger gameplay.
