Common questions

FAQ

Do I need prior milsim experience?

Not at all. Coalition is built around accessibility. We believe in baptism by fire and the best way to learn is to mess up.

Is there a minimum age requirement?

Yes — Coalition is an 18+ community. This helps keep the tone mature and the environment welcoming for everyone.

Do I have to attend every event?

No mandatory attendance. Play when you want, slot into the roles you enjoy, and come back whenever you like. There's no commitment required.

Can I play on console?

We are currently a PC only community.

Do I need to signup?

No, only events need to be signed up for. Fight Club (CFC) and Sessions are free to join/leave any time.
